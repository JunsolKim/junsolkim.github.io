Junsol Kim

PhD student in Sociology

University of Chicago

I am a second-year PhD student in Sociology at the University of Chicago advised by Professors James Evans and Byungkyu Lee. Previously, I received my BA/MA in Sociology and BS in Computer Science at Yonsei University. My works have appeared in Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS) and several other outlets. You can find my CV here.

My research interests include

  1. Computational Social Science and Natural Language Processing: How can social scientists leverage generative AI to simulate human behaviors and augment human subjects?

  2. Social Networks, Neuroscience and Health: How does human brain process social networks? How do social networks impact cognitive health?

  3. Political Sociology: How does misinformation diffuse in the polarized information landscape? How can we intervene?
